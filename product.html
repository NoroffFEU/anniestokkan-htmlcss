PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Introducing the Willow Light Down Rain Jacket, the epitome of style, functionality, and comfort combined in one exceptional outerwear piece. Imagine embracing the beauty of nature in all its splendor while staying impeccably dry and cozy.

Designed to repel raindrops effortlessly, this jacket boasts a lightweight yet insulating down filling, ensuring you're snug and warm no matter the weather. The elegant silhouette and thoughtful detailing make it a fashion-forward choice for any adventure or city stroll. With its durability and versatility, the Willow Light Down Rain Jacket is more than just a coat—it's a statement of sophistication and readiness for whatever the elements may bring.

Elevate your outdoor experiences and stay chic in the rain with the Willow Light Down Rain Jacket your perfect companion for rainy days and beyond.

MATERIALS AND MAINTENANCE

  • Shell: 100% polyester
  • Lining: 100% nylon
  • Down: 100% grey goose down
  • Composition: 85% down/15% down
  • Fillpower: 700 cuin
  • Weight main fabric: 100gsm
  • Weight sleeves: 60 gsm
  • Maintenance: Machine wash cold, gentle cycle 30°C. Close zippers and wash inside out. Wash with similar colours. Don't use fabric softener or bleach. Rinse thoroughly. Do not wring. Do not dry clean. Tumble dry low together with 3 tennis balls. Iron on low temperatures.
  • Consider the environment - don't overwash!
